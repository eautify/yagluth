Humidity and Temperature Sensor

This activity involves monitoring temperature and humidity levels over time using a DHT sensor.

Humidity

Dry (0% - 30%):

Very low moisture.


Comfortable (31% - 50%):

Ideal moisture level.


Humid (51% - 70%):

Noticeable moisture in the air.


Very Humid (71% and above):

Sticky and heavy air.
