Gas and Vibration Sensor

This activity focuses on using an MQ-2 gas sensor for gas detection and SW-420 vibration sensor to monitor movement.

MQ-2 Gas Sensor

--

--

Normal (below 20 ppm):

Gas concentration is within safe levels.


Warning (20 - 60 ppm):

Gas concentration is approaching a potentially unsafe level.


Danger (above 60 ppm):

Gas concentration has reached a critical level and requires immediate action.

SW-420 Vibration Sensor

--

No Vibration Detected:

The sensor is not detecting any vibrations or movement, indicating a stable environment.


Vibration Detected:

The sensor has detected vibrations, signaling potential motion or disturbances in the environment. Immediate attention may be required to assess the situation.
