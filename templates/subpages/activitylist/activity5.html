Sound and Raindrop Sensor

This activity involves monitoring sound levels using a sound sensor and detecting moisture with a raindrop sensor.

Sound Sensor (KY-037)

--

--

--

Quiet (0 - 50 dB):

Low-level sounds, such as soft music or distant conversations.

Moderate (51 - 90 dB):

Noticeable sounds, like normal conversations or background noise.

Very Loud (91 - 150 dB):

Extremely loud noise, like shouting or heavy machinery, indicating a critical level.

Raindrop Sensor (YL-83)

--

--

--

No Rain (0):

No raindrops detected.

Light Rain (1 - 10):

A few raindrops detected, indicating a light drizzle.

Moderate Rain (11 - 25):

Consistent raindrops, suggesting moderate rainfall.

Heavy Rain (26 - 50):

Heavy rainfall is detected.

Intense Rain (51 - 80):

Intense rainfall detected. Prepare for significant rain.

Torrential Rain (81 - 100):

The sensor is nearly fully wet, indicating torrential downpour.
